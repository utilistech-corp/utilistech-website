How It Works

From first call to full deployment.

A focused 60-minute conversation to understand your business, your current processes, and where the friction is. No pitch — just questions and listening. Free, no obligation.

02
Audit & Scoping

We map your workflows, assess your data landscape, and identify the highest-impact AI opportunities. You get a written report with a proposed scope, timeline, and fixed cost — before any work begins.
