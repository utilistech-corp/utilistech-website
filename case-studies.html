Case Studies

Real results for real businesses.

Every project starts with a problem worth solving. Here's how we've helped UK organisations across different sectors adopt AI safely, understand the impact, and actually use it.

💼

Financial Services

Mid-size advisory firm, ~50 employees, London

The Challenge

Wanted to adopt AI but worried about compliance, data handling, and where to start. No clear roadmap or understanding of which processes were suitable for automation.

Our Approach

Half-day discovery workshop, process mapping across core workflows, data readiness review, and a prioritised roadmap. We identified governance requirements and compliance considerations upfront.

Results

Clear 6-month AI adoption plan delivered
3 high-impact processes identified for automation
Governance framework in place before any AI tools deployed
Team confidence increased — ready to move forward safely
⏱️ Timeline: 2 weeks
⚖️

Professional Services

Legal firm, ~120 employees, Manchester

The Challenge

Associates spending 15+ hours per week manually reviewing and summarising client documents. Inconsistent output, human error, and a bottleneck affecting fee earner utilisation.

Our Approach

Built a custom AI tool that reads incoming documents, categorises them by type, extracts key facts, and produces summaries with human-in-the-loop review. Full audit trail for compliance and client confidence.

Results

60% reduction in document processing time
Consistent output quality — no more variation between staff
Full audit trail for compliance and client review
Associates freed up for higher-value work
⏱️ Timeline: 6 weeks
🛍️

Retail & E-Commerce

E-commerce retailer, ~30 employees, Birmingham

The Challenge

Team had heard about AI but had no idea how to use it safely or effectively. No clear policies, worried about data risks, and uncertain which tools were appropriate for their work.

Our Approach

Full-day training workshop with hands-on exercises using real workflows. Created an approved tools list and AI usage policy aligned with data safety requirements. Follow-up sessions to measure adoption and identify new opportunities.

Results

80% of team actively using AI tools within 4 weeks
Clear data safety guidelines embedded in everyday practice
2 new process automations identified by staff themselves
Confidence and momentum to continue improving
⏱️ Timeline: 1 day workshop + 2 weeks follow-up
