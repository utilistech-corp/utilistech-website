AI Email Assistant

Intelligent Email Drafting & Response

Your team spends hours every day reading, categorising, and drafting email responses. Repetitive enquiries consume skilled staff time that should be spent on higher-value work. The AI Email Assistant connects to your existing email system — it reads incoming email, understands intent, drafts a professional reply in your brand voice, and sends it for one-click approval before anything goes out.

How it works
1

Incoming email arrives in your shared mailbox or designated inbox.

2

AI reads and classifies the email — enquiry type, urgency, and sentiment.

3

A draft reply is generated using your tone of voice, templates, and business rules.

4

Your team reviews via Teams notification — approve, edit, or reject with one click.

5

Approved reply sent from your mailbox automatically. Full audit trail kept.

Solution 02

AI Document Intelligence

Intelligent Document Analysis & Data Extraction

Businesses store documents across shared drives and folders and then can't find or act on anything. Contracts renew without notice, invoices go overdue, and critical obligations buried in supplier agreements are missed entirely. The service connects to your document storage and automatically reads every new document — extracting key data and writing it straight back as structured metadata. No new tools. No manual tagging.

How it works
1

A new document is uploaded to your monitored SharePoint library — contracts, invoices, proposals, or HR documents.

2

AI reads the full document and extracts key data: dates, parties, monetary values, renewal clauses, and obligations.

3

SharePoint metadata columns are automatically populated — document type, category, tags, key dates, and contract values.

4

Alerts are evaluated daily — upcoming renewals, expiring contracts, and overdue invoices are flagged automatically.

5

A Teams notification is posted with a summary and actionable next steps for your team.

Solution 03

AI Meeting Assistant

Intelligent Meeting Summaries & Action Tracking

Teams meetings generate notes nobody reads and actions nobody tracks. Key decisions are forgotten, follow-ups slip through the cracks, and staff waste time writing up summaries that rarely reach the people who need them. The AI Meeting Assistant automatically processes meeting transcripts — extracting clean summaries, action items, and key decisions, then posting them to Teams and creating tasks in Planner without anyone lifting a finger.

How it works
1

A Teams meeting ends and the transcript is captured automatically.

2

AI reads the full transcript — identifying topics discussed, decisions made, and actions assigned.

3

A clean summary with structured action items is generated in your brand voice.

4

The summary is posted to your Teams channel and emailed to all attendees automatically.

5

Action items are created as tasks in Planner or Microsoft To Do, assigned to the right people.

Management teamsProject meetings Client callsBoard meetings 5+ meetings per week
